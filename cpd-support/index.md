# CPD Progress Tracker – Support

If you need help using CPD Progress Tracker, please contact us using the details below.

## Contact Support

**Email:** bmbapps@icloud.com  
We aim to respond within 48 hours.

## Common Help Topics

### • Problems adding or editing CPD entries
Make sure you are using the latest version of the app. Try restarting the app or your device.

### • QR code scanning not working
Ensure the code is well lit and fully visible in the camera frame. Try cleaning the camera lens.

### • Issues exporting or sharing your CPD
Check that you have allowed the app permission to access your photos/files.  
Try exporting again after closing and reopening the app.

### • Unable to attach photos or documents
Ensure you have granted the app permission to access your Files or Photos library.

## Additional Information
- CPD Progress Tracker stores your data locally on your device.  
- You can export your information at any time using the built-in export tools.  
- No third-party content or external services are used.

If you continue to have issues, please email us and we will be happy to assist.
